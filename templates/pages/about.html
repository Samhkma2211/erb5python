Our Story

Love your life and love your pets!

In 2009, Clara Chan, a lifelong animal lover, found herself standing in the rain outside a shivering, abandoned golden retriever puppy. She took him home, named him Sunny, and nursed him back to health. But as she visited local shelters to find Sunny's siblings, she was heartbroken by the overcrowded facilities and the lack of resources for pet owners struggling to care for their animals.
