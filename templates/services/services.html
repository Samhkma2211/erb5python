Veterinary Services

Veterinarians specialize in different areas of animal care. Knowing their roles helps pet owners access the right care for their pets.

Choosing the right vet for your pet
  • General-practice veterinarians: Provide routine check-ups, vaccinations, dental care and general treatment for illnesses and injuries.
  • Emergency vets: Provide 24/7 care outside regular clinic hours. Emergency veterinarians are trained to handle urgent situations
  • Specialist veterinarians: Provide advanced diagnostic procedures, specialised treatments, and consultations for complex cases.
